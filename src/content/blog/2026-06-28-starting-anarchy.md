This is the first entry in the **Anarchy** devlog.

Anarchy is a game I'm making under Bridge Apps. It's early — more idea than
game right now — and this blog is where I'll think out loud as it takes shape:
what I'm building, what's working, what isn't, and the decisions along the way.

## Why a devlog

Writing things down forces me to be honest about where the project actually is.
It also leaves a trail — for me to look back on, and for anyone curious about
how the thing got made.

## What to expect

- Short, frequent notes over polished essays.
- Screenshots and prototypes once there's something to show.
- The occasional dead end, kept in for the record.

More soon.
